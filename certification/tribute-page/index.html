Alfredo Toronito

The visionary founder of dockVind

Here is Alfredo Toronito's journey

  • 1949 - Born Alfredo Zoorasthmus Cunningham Toronito in the erstwhile state of Hyderabad to American immigrants
  • 1967 - Leaves India to attend Harvard where he is admitted into a BS program
  • 1971 - graduates from college and joins as a researcher at Bell Labs
  • 1999 - founds dockVind in his birth town Hyderabad
  • 2010 - Under his leadership, dockVind becomes a Fortune 500 company
  • 2015 - resigns from his position as chairman to focus on philanthropic activities
  • 2022 - passes away at the age of 73
